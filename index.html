Story

1. Displaying Age and Gender of the Participants








The Heat Map is plotted to visualize the Participant’s details. Here, the age and gender of the participants are taken for displaying. So, it is visualized that Females and Males of the age group 18-22 are more in number.

2. Which Gender misses Shopping more?








This Bar Graph is plotted to visualize whether Male/Female misses the shopping more. Symbols have been used to differentiate between male and female gender. It is visualized that Females miss shopping more than Male.

3. Displaying the people who has maximum screen time








This Horizontal Bar Graph is plotted to visualize the screen time of the participants. The number of participants is arranged in ascending order. It is visualized that most people have a screen time of 4-8 hrs.

4. Percentage of people who felt they haven't changed during the Pandemic








This Pie Chart is plotted to visualize the changes felt by the participants (in %). The changes are differentiated by colors. It is visualized that most of the participants felt that they have evolved greatly as an individual.

5. Age-wise Analysis of people who do not feel connected as a group in online








This Bar Graph is plotted to visualize whether Male/Female misses the shopping more. Symbols have been used to differentiate between male and female gender. It is visualized that Females miss shopping more than Male.

6. Age-wise Chatting Habits of people based on their Accommodation








This Bubble Chart is plotted to visualize age-wise chatting habits of people on the basis of where they live. The chart is differentiated by age (Color) and the size of the circle is decided by the Chatting Habits (Sum). It is visualized that people who are in the age-group of 18-22 and living with their family spend more time Chatting.

7. Moods of the Participants during the Lockdown








This Pie Chart is plotted to visualize the changes felt by the participants (in %). The changes are differentiated by colors. It is visualized that most of the participants felt that they have evolved greatly as an individual.

8. Participant’s feelings towards the COVID-19 Pandemic








This Pie Chart is plotted to visualize the feelings of the participants during the COVID-19 Pandemic. The chart is differentiated by Feelings (Color) and the angle depends on the number of people. It is visualized that most of the people are “Hopeful that life will return to normal after the pandemic”.

9. Concerns of the participants during the Pandemic (Gender-wise)








The horizontal stacked bar chart to visualize the concerns of participants during the pandemic. The chart is differentiated by Females and Males by concerns (Color). It is visualized that Females are concerned more about Examination.

10. Time spent on collecting COVID-19 Information based on their Profession








The Symbol Chart is plotted to visualize to collect information about the pandemic based on their profession. The chart is differentiated by Profession (Symbol) and participants who spends time in collecting information about COVID-19. It is visualized that College student collect information more in 1/2hr to 1hr duration.

About the Dataset

    Dataset: Locked Up In a Lockdown.
    Source: Mendeley Data and A small survey conducted by the team through Google Forms.
    Year: 2019 – 2021.
    No. of columns: 30.
    No. of rows: 841.
    Description: The team has asked some questions about the participant’s opinion about various things that happened in their lives during the Pandemic and recorded their responses in an excel-sheet format. Charts/Graphs will be plotted to view the outcome of the Survey.
